Forex Fund Recovery – GCC (Meta Ads)
Meta lead campaigns for a legal recovery service helping victims in KSA, Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE, Qatar and Oman reclaim funds from scam Forex brokers, using compliant messaging and high‑intent lead forms.

Campaign Overview

Objective: Generate qualified consultation requests for a law / recovery firm that helps clients recover stolen or blocked funds from fraudulent Forex companies.

Markets & audience: Gulf countries: KSA, Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE, Qatar and Oman. Arabic-speaking adults 25–60 who previously invested in Forex / online trading and report problems withdrawing funds. Custom and lookalike audiences built from past recovery leads and clients.

Strategy & Setup

Account structure:

  • Separate ad sets per GCC country to control budget, bids and CPL by market.
  • All campaigns optimized to Leads (Form) with qualifying questions about loss amount and broker.

Creatives & messaging:

  • Clear Arabic copy focused on legal help, rights and transparency (not investment promises).
  • Angles tested: “don’t lose hope”, “free legal consultation”, “no win, no fee” and “specialized Forex recovery team”.
  • Visuals: serious legal / financial imagery to build trust and avoid looking like another scam.

Optimization approach:

  • Weekly review of CPL and lead quality feedback from the legal team.
  • Pausing high‑CPL or low‑quality ad sets and reallocating budget to best‑performing countries and creatives.
  • Frequency control to avoid over‑exposure in smaller GCC markets.

Results Snapshot

  • Total leads: 222+ recovery leads across all GCC markets.
  • Total spend: ≈ 3,120 USD on Meta Ads.
  • Average CPL: ≈ 14.1 USD per lead.
  • Delivery: 180K+ impressions and 68K+ reach, building strong visibility for the recovery brand in the region.

These Meta campaigns consistently generated high-intent recovery leads from Forex scam victims across the GCC at an average CPL of around 14 USD, feeding the legal team with qualified consultation requests.

Key Learnings

  • Pain-focused, empathetic messaging (“recover your money”, “legal support”) outperformed generic anti-scam messages.
  • Strong pre-qualification inside the lead form (loss amount, broker name, country) saved the legal team time and improved case quality.
  • Splitting countries allowed shifting more budget into markets with both good CPL and higher average claim size.

My Role

  • Designed the overall GCC recovery strategy on Meta: markets, structure, targeting and messaging.
  • Built and managed all campaigns, including writing Arabic ad copy and coordinating with designers on creatives.
  • Monitored results daily, optimized budgets and audiences, and reported CPL, lead volume and case quality back to the recovery firm.
