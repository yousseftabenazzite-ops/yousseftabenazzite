Forex Lead Generation – GCC (Meta Ads)
Always‑on Meta campaigns generating qualified Forex leads across KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain and Oman.

Campaign Overview

Objective: Generate a consistent flow of high‑intent leads for a Forex brokerage across multiple GCC countries using Meta lead forms and localized creatives.

Markets: KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman.

Target audience: Men and women 24–55 interested in trading, Forex, investing and side income, plus lookalikes built from high‑quality leads and depositors.

Strategy & Setup

Account & structure:

  • Always‑on structure with separate ad sets per country to control budget and CPL.
  • Mix of lead form campaigns and traffic to landing pages depending on country compliance.

Creatives & messaging:

  • Multiple angles: regulated broker, welcome bonus, education‑first and multi‑asset trading.
  • A/B tests on Arabic vs English copy, static vs motion, and different value propositions (low spreads, fast withdrawals, local support).

Optimization approach:

  • Optimization to “Leads (form)” with 7‑day click attribution.
  • Weekly pruning of under‑performing ads and reallocating budget to best markets and creatives.
  • Negative audiences to exclude existing clients and low‑quality segments.

Results Snapshot

  • Total leads: 864 qualified Forex leads across all GCC ad sets.
  • Total spend: 6,398 USD invested on Meta Ads.
  • Average CPL: ≈ 7.40 USD per lead across six countries.
  • Delivery: 1M+ impressions and 400K+ reach, building awareness and a steady pipeline.

These Meta campaigns delivered 864 qualified Forex leads across KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain and Oman at an average CPL of around 7.4 USD, providing a predictable pipeline of prospects for the brokerage’s sales team.

Key Learnings

  • Country‑level separation made it easy to shift budget into the best‑performing markets without losing volume elsewhere.
  • Arabic creatives with clear, compliant messaging outperformed aggressive “get rich quick” angles and improved lead quality.
  • Lead forms with qualifying questions reduced junk leads and helped the sales team focus on real trading intent.

My Role

  • Full ownership of Meta campaigns: planning, setup and daily optimization.
  • Writing ad copy and coordinating with designers on creatives.
  • Weekly reporting on CPL, volume and country performance, with clear scaling and optimization recommendations.
