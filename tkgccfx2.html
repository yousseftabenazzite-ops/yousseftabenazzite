Forex Lead Generation – GCC (TikTok Ads – Account 2)
Second TikTok account targeting the same GCC markets with new creatives and audiences. Generated 814 leads at 7.46 USD CPL with full coverage across UAE, KSA, Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait and Qatar.

Campaign Overview

Objective: Scale TikTok lead generation for the same GCC Forex broker using a second account with new creatives, audiences and testing approach to increase total volume.

Markets & audience: UAE, KSA, Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait, Qatar. Arabic-speaking young adults 21–45 interested in Forex trading, investing and side income opportunities.

Strategy & Setup

Account structure:

  • Lead Generation campaigns with country‑specific Ad groups for UAE, KSA, Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait and Qatar.
  • Optimization to Leads with TikTok Pixel and event tracking.

Creatives & messaging:

  • New batch of 15–25 second Arabic videos testing different hooks and styles.
  • Focus on “education + simplicity” messaging: "Trade Forex in 3 steps", "Start with $100", "Licensed broker for GCC traders".
  • Mix of UGC, screen recordings and simple motion graphics optimized for TikTok's algorithm.

Optimization approach:

  • Daily testing of hooks, first 3 seconds, thumbnails and CTAs.
  • Budget reallocation to best‑performing countries and creatives based on CPL and lead quality.
  • Continuous audience refinement using lookalikes from high‑performing leads.

Results Snapshot

  • Total spend: 6,070+ USD across all GCC campaigns.
  • Total leads: 814 qualified Forex leads from UAE, KSA, Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait and Qatar.
  • Average CPL: 7.46 USD per lead.
  • Delivery: Strong impressions and reach across all six GCC countries.

This second TikTok account successfully scaled lead volume while maintaining efficient CPL, proving the channel's scalability for GCC Forex lead generation.

Key Learnings

  • Multiple accounts allow testing different creative approaches and audiences simultaneously.
  • Consistent "education-first" messaging works across both TikTok accounts and markets.
  • Country‑specific optimization prevents smaller markets from being drowned out by larger ones like KSA and UAE.

My Role

  • Creative strategy, video script writing and production coordination.
  • Daily optimization across bids, budgets, audiences and creatives with performance reporting to client.
