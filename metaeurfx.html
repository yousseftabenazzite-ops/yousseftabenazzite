Forex Lead Generation – Arab Diaspora in Europe (Meta Ads)
Ongoing Meta lead campaigns targeting Arabic‑speaking residents across multiple European countries. Focused on compliant Forex messaging, localized creatives and form optimization to drive a stable volume of qualified trading leads

Campaign Overview

Objective: Generate high-intent Forex leads from Arabic-speaking residents living in Europe for a regulated brokerage, using Meta lead forms and Arabic-first creatives.

Markets & audience: Arabic-speaking residents in Germany, Sweden, Spain, Italy, Switzerland, Netherlands, United Kingdom, Austria and Belgium. Age 25–55, interested in trading, Forex, investing and side income opportunities. Lookalike audiences built from existing high-quality leads and first-time depositors.

Strategy & Setup

Account structure:

  • Separate campaigns by country or small country clusters to control budget and CPL by market.
  • All campaigns optimized for Leads (Form) with clear qualifying questions.

Creatives & messaging:

  • Arabic-language ads explaining the benefits of trading with a regulated broker while respecting EU compliance rules.
  • Angles tested: capital growth, diversification, education-first, and “trade from anywhere in Europe”.
  • Mix of static images, short motion creatives and simple explainers that look native inside the feed.

Optimization approach:

  • Continuous A/B testing on headlines, primary text and creatives.
  • Scaling budgets only on ad sets that delivered both good CPL and strong lead-quality feedback from the sales team.
  • Tight frequency control to avoid fatigue in smaller diaspora audiences.

Results Snapshot

  • Total leads: 1,350+ qualified Forex leads from Arabic-speaking audiences in Europe.
  • Total spend: around 13,750 USD across all campaigns.
  • Average CPL: approximately 10.2 USD per lead.
  • Delivery: strong reach and impressions across multiple European markets while keeping CPL within brokerage targets.

These Meta campaigns helped the brokerage tap into the Arabic-speaking diaspora in Europe, generating over 1,150 qualified leads at an average CPL of about 12 USD while staying compliant with EU advertising rules.

Key Learnings

  • Narrow language + interest targeting was more effective than broad country targeting alone for this niche audience.
  • Clear compliance-friendly copy in Arabic outperformed aggressive promises and resulted in better lead quality and lower refund/complaint risk.
  • Country-level campaign separation allowed shifting budget to the most scalable markets without losing presence in smaller countries.

My Role

  • Planned the cross-country targeting and campaign structure for the Arab diaspora segment in Europe.
  • Built and launched all Meta campaigns, including ad copywriting and coordination on creatives.
  • Monitored performance daily, optimized budgets and audiences, and provided regular reports on CPL, volume and lead quality to the client.
