Complaint Lead Generation – GCC (Google Ads)
Google Ads campaigns for complaint and claim services targeting GCC countries, using high-intent search keywords and optimized landing pages to capture qualified leads with strong conversion performance.

Campaign Overview

Objective: Generate high-quality complaint and claim leads from GCC countries using Google Ads, focusing on users actively searching for legal help, compensation, and dispute resolution.

Markets & audience: GCC countries including KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman. Users searching for terms related to complaints, financial disputes, fund recovery, and legal assistance.

Strategy & Setup

Campaign structure:

  • Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords such as “file complaint”, “financial claim”, and “fund recovery”.
  • Segmentation by service type and keyword clusters to control budget and performance.
  • Conversion-focused bidding strategy (Maximize Conversions).

Landing pages:

  • Simple and clear pages focused on trust, authority, and fast form submission.
  • Strong Arabic and English CTAs like “Submit your complaint now” and “Get a free consultation”.

Optimization approach:

  • Continuous keyword optimization (adding negatives and scaling high-performing queries).
  • Budget reallocation toward campaigns with better conversion rates.
  • Monitoring cost per conversion and improving lead quality over time.

Results Snapshot

  • Total impressions: 75,000+ impressions.
  • Total conversions: 17 confirmed leads.
  • Total spend: around 3,445 MAD.
  • Average CPL: approximately 200 MAD per lead.

The campaigns successfully captured high-intent users from search, generating consistent complaint leads with controlled costs and a strong focus on quality over volume.

Key Learnings

  • High-intent search traffic delivers better lead quality compared to social channels.
  • Keyword precision and negative keywords are critical to control CPL.
  • Simple, trust-focused landing pages significantly improve conversion rates.
